What is CSS?

Cascading Style Sheet (CSS) is used to format the lay out of a webpage
With CSS, you can control the color, font, the size of text, the spacing between elements, how element are positioned and laid out, what background images or background colors are to used, different displays for different devices and screen sizes, and much more!
Tip: The word cascading means that a style applied to a parent elment will also apply to a children elements within the parent.So, if you set the color of the body text to blue, all headings, paragraphs, and other text element within the body will also get the same color(unless you specify something else)
